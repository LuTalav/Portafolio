  <h1>CHILAQUILES</h1>

  <p>A delicious and spicy Mexican breakfast dish.</p>

  <h2> Ingredients</h2>
  <ul>
  <li>4 corn tortillas, cut into triangles</li>
  <li>1 cup of green or red salsa</li>
  <li>1/2 cup of shredded chicken (optional)</li>
  <li>1/4 cup of sour cream</li>
  </ul>
  <h2>Instructions</h2>
<ol>
  <li>Heat oil in a pan and fry the tortilla pieces until crispy.</li>
  <li>In a separate pan, heat the salsa until it simmers.</li>
  <li>Add the fried tortilla pieces to the salsa and stir until well-coated.</li>
  <li>Top with shredded chicken, sour cream, cheese, and a fried egg (optional).</li>
  <li>Garnish with chopped onions, avocado, and cilantro.</li>
  <li>Serve and enjoy your delicious Chilaquiles!</li>
</ol>
